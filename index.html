    <h2>Oreo Cheesecake (is the best) recipe</h2>
        <p><mark>2 pkg. (8 oz. each)</mark> brick cream cheese, softened<br>
            <mark>1/2 cup</mark> sugar<br>
            <mark>1/2 tsp.</mark> vanilla<br>
            <mark>2</mark> eggs<br>
            <mark>6</mark> OREO Cookies, coarsely chopped, divided,<br>
            <mark>1</mark> OREO Pie Crust (6 oz.)</p>
        <p> Instructions:<br> <mark>HEAT</mark>  oven to 325 degrees F.<br>
            <mark>BEAT</mark>  cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with mixer until blended. Add eggs; mix just until blended. Stir in 1/2 cup cookies.<br>
            <mark>POUR</mark>  into crust; sprinkle with remaining cookies.<br>
            <mark>BAKE</mark>  35 to 40 min. or until center is almost set. Cool. Refrigerate 3 hours.</p>
